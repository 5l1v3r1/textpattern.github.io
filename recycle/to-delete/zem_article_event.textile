h4(#classification). Classification

The

tag can be used as a "Single&#95;Tag":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=glossary#single_tag or a "Container Tag":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=glossary#container_tag. When used as a "Container Tag":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=glossary#container_tag the contained statements are executed recursively as the "Container Tag":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=glossary#container_tag's form.
