*_TextBook_* is a community-produced user manual for "Textpattern":http://www.textpattern.com, and compliments the Textpattern "FAQ":http://textpattern.com/faq/. Where the FAQ is brief, TextBook aims to be more explanatory, as well as provide articles in multiple languages. To get started writing or editing articles, obtain a wiki account via "new_accounts":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=new_accounts. Please offer content suggestions and feedback in Textpattern's "Documentation Discussion":http://forum.textpattern.com/viewforum.php?id=28 forum.
