h1. Extensions

The **Extensions** region is not a core region you see out-of-the-box. It appears when you add certain plugins that make use of it. Plugins will do this when they provide configurable functionality specific to their design and use.

When you upload such a plugin and activate it, the **Extensions** region appears on all admin-side views _except_ when you're in context of the **Plugins** panel itself. This ensures buggy (e.g. outdated) plugins can be deactivated safely even if the rest of the site manifests problems due to the buggy plugin.
