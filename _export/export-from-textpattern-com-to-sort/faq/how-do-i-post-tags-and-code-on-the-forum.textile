h1. How do I post tags and code on the forum? [todo]

For inline code:

bc. @<txp:permlink><txp:title /></txp:permlink>@

p. For short snippets:

bc. bc. <h3><txp:permlink><txp:title /></txp:permlink></h3>
<p><txp:posted /></p>

p. For longer snippets:

bc.. bc.. <txp:permlink><txp:title /></txp:permlink>
<p><txp:posted /></p>
	
<txp:body />

p. To post short Textile examples:

bc. ==*Bold*==

or

bc. notextile. *Bold*

Or, for longer Textile examples:

bc.. notextile.. h1. My Title

*Bold*

p. If you're still having trouble, or posting something long or complex, consider posting your code on "pastebin.com":http://pastebin.com/ and linking to that content from the forum.
