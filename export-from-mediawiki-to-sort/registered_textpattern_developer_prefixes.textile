\nA list of plugin author prefixes already in use, and who they belong to (support forum username).

The convention is to register *only one* three-character (alphanumeric) prefix for _all_ of your Textpattern products (themes and plugins). *It's appreciated if everyone follows this simple guideline.*

Some authors have more than one prefix because they “adopted” plugins no longer maintained by the creator.
