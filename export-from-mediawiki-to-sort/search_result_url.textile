h3(sectionedit1#attributes). Attributes

This tag has no attributes.

h3(sectionedit2#examples). Examples

h4(#example_1display_a_hyperlinked_url_to_an_article). Example 1: Display a hyperlinked URL to an article

Used within a search results form to allow visitors to click on the link and be taken to the article that matched their search results.
