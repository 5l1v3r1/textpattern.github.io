The "article&#95;id":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=article_id tag is a _single_ tag which returns the numeric ID of the article being displayed. This number will also be reflected as a part of the article permanent URL if it has been chosen as the _Permanent link mode_ in the "basic&#95;preferences":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=basic_preferences tab.

h3(sectionedit1#attributes). Attributes

This tag has no attributes.

h3(sectionedit2#examples). Examples

h4(#example_1hyperlinked_to_the_article). Example 1: Hyperlinked to the article

bc(code). <txp:permlink><txp:article_id /></txp:permlink>
