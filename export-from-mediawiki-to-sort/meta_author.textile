Used in the head of an individual article page template, the "meta&#95;author":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=meta_author tag is a _single_ tag. Textpattern will replace this tag with an HTML meta tag as follows:

bc(code). <meta name="author" content="''Article author's name''" />
