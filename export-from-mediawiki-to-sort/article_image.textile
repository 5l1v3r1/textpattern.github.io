h3(sectionedit2#examples). Examples

h4(#example_1use_wraptag_and_class_for_styling). Example 1: Use wraptag and class for styling

bc(code). <txp:article_image wraptag="p" class="article-image" />


*What this does...*
 This will wrap the image in paragraph tags, applying the class to the paragraph:

bc(code). <nowiki><p class="article-image"><img src="..." /></p></nowiki>


. *Why you might do it...*
 It gives you full control over the image's appearance using CSS. *Note*
 Without the wraptag, the class is applied directly to the

bc(code). img


tag
