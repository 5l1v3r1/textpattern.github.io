h3(sectionedit1#using_subversion_svn). Using Subversion (SVN)

Textpattern is developed under "Subversion":/home/www/zendstudio/dokuwiki/bin/doku.php?id=glossary#subversion (SVN). Subversion is a wonderful versioning system (quickly supplanting CVS) that makes it easy for development, graphics, and writing teams to collaborate while ensuring their work is saved and managable at every step. Aside from the regular Textpattern ZIP download, you can also install Textpattern using one of the following subversion methods, therefore taking advantage of the benefits subversion provides.
