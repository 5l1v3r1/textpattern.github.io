h1. Search result url

h2. Syntax

bc. <txp:search_result_url />

The *search_result_url* tag is a _single_ tag. This tag will provide a hyperlinked URL to an article as returned by the search function.

h2. Attributes

This tag has no attributes.

h2. Examples

h3. Example 1: Display a hyperlinked URL to an article

bc. <txp:search_result_url />

Used within a search results form to allow visitors to click on the link and be taken to the article that matched their search results.
