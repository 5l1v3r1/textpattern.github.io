h1. Next title

h2. Syntax

bc. <txp:next_title />

The *next_title* tag is a _single_ tag which Textpattern will replace with the title of the next article in the sort order.

The container tag "link_to_next":link-to-next.textile wraps the text or tag and assigns the link.

h2. Attributes

This tag has no attributes.

h2. Examples

h3. Example 1: Link to next article by its title

bc. <txp:link_to_next>
    <txp:next_title />
</txp:link_to_next>

Other tags used: "link_to_next":link-to-next.textile.
