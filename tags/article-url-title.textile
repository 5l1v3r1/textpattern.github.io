h1. Article url title

h2. Syntax

bc. <txp:article_url_title />

The *article_url_title* tag is a _single_ tag which returns the dumbed-down 'URL title' of the article being displayed. This URL title may also be part of the page's address depending on the 'Permanent link mode' chosen in the [[Preferences page]].

h2. Attributes

This tag has no attributes.
